WHY 90% GOES UNREPORTED

Three barriers stop victims

1. "Is this even illegal?"
Victims can't tell if a comment is § 185 (insult, 1 year) or § 241 (threat, 2 years).
Legal literacy shouldn't be required to report a crime.

2. "My evidence disappeared"
Posts get deleted. Screenshots aren't timestamped. When victims go to police, evidence is gone.

3. "It's too complicated"
NetzDG forms buried in settings. Police need structured reports. Filing takes hours. Most give up.
