Second AI layer: from classification to legal PDF

This week’s task was to make the second AI layer useful in the final legal document, not just produce another intermediate AI output.

Step 1
Classifier
Evidence is structured by severity, category, and applicable law.
Step 2
Legal AI
The case-level layer produces legal assessment, strongest charges, actions, risk, and evidence gaps.
Step 3
Legal PDF
The final export now includes the second AI layer in a visible and auditable way.
Main change
The legal analysis is now part of the final PDF
The output is stored as a case analysis row and then surfaced in the export, instead of remaining a detached backend result.
Note
ReportLab is the PDF stack
I did not switch to pypdf. ReportLab is sufficient here because the main need is generating final legal documents, not manipulating existing PDFs.
The key change is simple: the second AI layer now reaches the actual document.
